Our Foundation

Built from Experience,
Not Just Theory.

I didn't start this company because it seemed like a good business opportunity on paper. I started it because I kept seeing the same problem everywhere I looked.

After years in the U.S. Army and then federal HR and executive administration, I moved into the private sector — and found small business owners drowning in the exact challenges I'd spent my career solving. Compliance issues with no one qualified to handle them. Workflows that only functioned because the right person happened to still be around. No documentation, no accountability structure, nothing that could survive real growth or scrutiny.

Mission Standard Advisory launched in 2026 to fix that. Founded by Emmanuel Abreu, the firm is headquartered in New Jersey and operates nationwide, serving clients primarily through remote consulting with in-person options throughout the Northeast.

Mission Standard Advisory targets a segment large firms consistently overlook: small and mid-sized business owners who know their operations need structure but lack the internal expertise to build it themselves.

Regulatory requirements at both the federal and state levels have become increasingly complex, creating real liability for businesses without formal compliance systems. The rise of remote and hybrid work has introduced new HR gray areas most small businesses haven't fully addressed.

Companies that get their systems right now will be better positioned to scale, survive audits, and avoid compliance failures. The long-term vision includes retainer-based compliance services, contract consultants, digital operational toolkits, and a boutique advisory practice serving both regional and federal clients.
